The Lunar Code

Trade With Honor

The market works best when everyone keeps exchanges clear, fair, and respectful.

I

Deal Clearly

State what you need, what you offer, and confirm before any exchange begins.

II

Respect Value

Keep trades fair and avoid pressuring other farmers into rushed decisions.

III

Honor Trust

Complete agreed trades and communicate quickly if something changes.

IV

Use the Right Halls

Post offers in the proper channels so the market stays easy to navigate.

V

Protect Members

Report suspicious behavior and let staff help resolve concerns calmly.

VI

Keep It Friendly

Every trade should strengthen the community, not create conflict.
